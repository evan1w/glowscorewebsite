BODY IMPACT SCORES

What the scores actually mean

Each food gets three scores out of 100 based on its nutritional profile, ingredient quality, and known physiological effects. Higher is always better.

L
Leanness
How well the food supports body composition — protein density, fiber content, and absence of empty calories.
S
Skin
Antioxidants, omega-3s, and the absence of pro-inflammatory seed oils that can trigger skin flares.
B
Bloat
Fermentable fibers, additives, and sodium levels that drive water retention and GI discomfort.
